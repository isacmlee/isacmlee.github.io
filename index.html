About Me

Hi, I am currently a data quality analyst @ Cedars-Sinai.

Healthcare data is highly complex and originates from a diverse range of sources and health systems, making it vulnerable to outdated processes, human error, and inaccurate reporting. My work focuses on enhancing the accuracy, consistency, and flow of various data, including but not limited to clinical data (e.g., medication orders, patient demographics, medical history, diagnoses), provider data (e.g., credentials, demographics, activity), ADT data (e.g., patient movement), medical coding data (e.g., ICD, CPT, HCPCS), insurance data (e.g. referrals, authorizations), and financial data (e.g., charges, reimbursements). By improving data accuracy and consistency, I help support regulatory compliance, financial integrity, operational efficiency, and quality reporting (e.g. Length of Stay, Readmission Rates, Mortality Rates), ensuring that leadership has the insights needed for decision-making while maintaining a high standard of patient care.
